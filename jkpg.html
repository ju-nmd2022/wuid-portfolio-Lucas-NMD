What can you do here?

You can enjoy nature at one of the surrounding lake. No better space to go with the family. Alternatively you can enjoy some hot, gay loving at the local cruising spot in Strömsbergs Naturreservat.


Places to go out?

There's the student union night club Aka near the train station. Also there's a karaoke bar called Hörnan.


Places to eat

Jungle Thai is the best place to eat in Jönköping with great asian food and service. Also, Aqua Dinner & Drinks is a very popular italian restaurant with lots of vegetarian and gluten-free options
