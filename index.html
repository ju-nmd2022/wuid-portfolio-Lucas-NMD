Why NMD?

I haven't been able to get a job in Sweden even though I have a university degree and 10+ years of experience in my field. I thought it was due to a language barrier. But, according to SVT, swedes are very racist and they published an article saying that immigrants from my region take in average 6 years to find a job in their profession. Also, a study from Lund University shows bias against immigrants: the researchers submitted identical assignments to be graded under different names, and found out that "Greta" would get a higher grade than "Ahmed" for the same paper. Furthermore, they elected a Sweden Democrat government on the last elections. I know that IT is probably the only sector where my level of swedish would not matter, but I am starting to think that it might not be enough for me to get a job.
